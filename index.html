About Me

Shafana Parveen A is an Assistant Professor and Public Speaker with a multidisciplinary academic background spanning Tamil, Computer Science, and History.

She holds MCA, M.Phil., MA (Tamil), and MA (History) qualifications. She currently serves at Hajee Karutha Rowther Howdia College, Uthamapalayam.

She is actively engaged in teaching, mentoring students, and promoting digital awareness through public speaking, especially under the theme “Healthy Internet, Healthy Indian.”
